Pad Kra Pao

Description

Pad Kra Pao is a popular Thai street food. It has a spicy and savory flavor and is usually served with rice. The dish is made with meat and holy basil.

Ingredients

  • Minced chicken or pork
  • Garlic
  • Thai chili
  • Holy basil leaves
  • Cooking oil
  • Fish sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • SugarSugar

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Add garlic and chili
  3. Stir-fry until fragrant
  4. Add minced meat
  5. Cook until the meat is done
  6. Add fish sauce, soy sauce, and sugar
  7. Stir well
  8. Add holy basil leaves
  9. Turn off the heat and serve with rice

Pad Thai

Description

It’s like the perfect combo of salty, sweet, spicy, and sour all in one dish. The noodles, the peanuts, the lime just so good! Have you ever tried making it yourself, or do you usually get it from a restaurant?

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Eggs
  • Tofu and/or shrimp
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water for 20 minutes
  2. Drain the noodles and set aside
  3. Crack 2 eggs into a bowl
  4. Add 2 tablespoons Pad Thai sauce
  5. Add 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  6. Add 1 tablespoon sugar
  7. Whisk everything together
  8. Heat the pan with oil on it
  9. Add chopped garlic and stir-fry briefly
  10. Add shrimp or chicken and cook until done
  11. Add the noodles to the pan
  12. Pour the egg and sauce mixture into the pan
  13. Stir everything together
  14. Add bean sprouts and green onions
  15. Fry for 2–3 minutes
  16. Don’t forget to flip and mix well
  17. Turn off the heat
  18. Serve with peanuts and lime

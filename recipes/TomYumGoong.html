Tom Yum Goong

Description

Tom Yum Shrimp is a famous Thai soup with a hot and sour flavor. It is made with shrimp, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, galangal, chili, and lime juice. The soup is aromatic, spicy, and refreshing.

Ingredients

  • shrimp
  • Mushrooms
  • lime juice
  • Thai chilies

Steps

  1. Boil water or stock in a pot
  2. Add lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves
  3. Boil for 3â€“5 minutes until fragrant
  4. Add mushrooms (if using)
  5. Add shrimp to the pot
  6. Cook until shrimp turn pink
  7. Add fish sauce and chili paste
  8. Turn off the heat
  9. Add lime juice
  10. Stir well
  11. Taste and adjust seasoning
  12. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot
